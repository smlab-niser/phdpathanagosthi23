PhD Summer Reading Group 2023

Welcome to PhD Pathana Gosthi, hosted by SM-LAB at the National Institute of Science Education and Research (NISER) in Odisha! This exciting event, taking place from June 12th to June 17th, 2023, brings together PhD students in Machine Learning for a week of knowledge sharing and collaboration.

The event will feature presentations from selected PhD students who will share their insights on recent papers from top conferences such as AAAI 2023, CVPR 2023, ICLR 2023, ICML 2022, KDD 2022, NeurIPS 2022, and SIGGRAPH 2022. Students will have the opportunity to suggest three papers of their choice while applying, and may be requested to present alternate papers if needed.

PhD Pathana Gosthi aims to foster learning, discussion, and collaboration among PhD students, providing them with a platform to showcase their research and engage in meaningful discussions with fellow peers. The event will create a conducive environment for intellectual exchange and networking, with participants provided with accommodation, food, and travel expenses.

This website will serve as a hub for information and updates about the event, as well as a platform for students to submit their CV and SOP for consideration. Join us for this enriching experience as we delve into the cutting-edge world of Machine Learning and explore the latest advancements in the field!

June 12th, 2023 to June 17th, 2023
